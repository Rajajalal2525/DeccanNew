Q1. Why choose DeccanRealty.com?
DeccanRealty.com is Bangalore’s leading real estate consultancy, catering to all property-related needs. It’s a digital platform where buyers, sellers, tenants, and agents can connect easily and efficiently. You can list, browse, and explore real estate opportunities seamlessly.
Q2. Which cities do you operate in?
We currently offer our services in Bangalore and nearby areas.
Q3. How can I book a site visit for a new project?
Booking a site visit is simple! Visit DeccanRealty.com, enter your name and phone number, and click "Request Info." That’s it!
Q4. Is a mobile number required to book a site visit?
Yes, providing a mobile number is mandatory.

Q6. How long does it take for my property to appear in trending listings?
Your property will be visible within 1-2 hours after a quick screening process to ensure quality and security.
Q7. What types of properties are listed on your website?
We list a wide range of rental flats, apartments, residential properties, and plots, including new, resale, and rental properties. Detailed videos are available for a better viewing experience.
Q8. What documents are required to sell my property?
Here are the essential documents for selling a property:
  • Allotment Letter
  • Sale deed
  • Sanctioned plan
  • Encumbrance certificate
  • Sale agreement
Q9. Who is responsible for paying stamp duty – the buyer or seller?
The buyer is responsible for paying stamp duty.
